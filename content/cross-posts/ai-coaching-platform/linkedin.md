**POST COPY:**

The average executive coach costs $500 per hour.

For most companies, that's a budget for one leader while everyone else gets a LinkedIn Learning subscription they'll never finish.

I saw this gap firsthand building Skill Refinery.

Fortune 500 companies pour millions into "leadership development" that amounts to generic workshops and personality assessments.

Meanwhile, individual contributors—the people actually doing the work—get nothing.

**AI coaching platforms are closing this gap.**

Not by replacing human coaches, but by delivering personalized guidance at a scale that was never economically viable before.

**The numbers are striking:**

📊 Traditional e-learning: 5–15% completion rates
📊 AI coaching platforms: 60–80% regular engagement

📊 Human coaching: $15,000–$50,000 per person annually
📊 AI coaching: 80–95% lower cost per employee

📊 Self-directed learning: Baseline goal achievement
📊 AI coaching: 3.2x higher likelihood of hitting stated goals

**But here's what actually matters:**

It's not about the technology.

It's about the relationship.

The best AI coaching platforms don't just deliver content. They:

→ Check in proactively
→ Remember what you discussed last month
→ Adapt to your communication style
→ Hold you accountable to your own goals
→ Escalate to humans when nuance matters

**One enterprise deployment (2,000-person tech company):**

✓ 73% engaged with the platform weekly
✓ 89% reported improved confidence in difficult conversations
✓ Manager effectiveness scores: +18%
✓ Internal promotion rate: +31%

ROI: $180K platform cost vs $1.2M estimated savings from reduced turnover

**The mistake most companies make:**

They treat AI coaching like fancy content delivery.

They measure course completions instead of behavior change.

They abandon it after 30 days because "adoption isn't high enough."

They miss that coaching relationships have different adoption curves than training courses.

**What works:**

✅ 90-day evaluation cycles
✅ Leading indicators before lagging outcomes
✅ Integration with human support (managers, mentors, HR)
✅ Customization by function, level, and career path
✅ Goals around behavior change, not content consumption

**The future is human-AI collaboration.**

AI handles the scale and consistency—the ongoing check-ins, skill-building, accountability.

Humans handle the depth and judgment—complex situations, career-defining moments, emotional support.

The question isn't whether AI coaching works.

The data is clear: it does.

The question is whether you're ready to scale development in a way that actually reaches your people.
