**The 5 systems every law firm needs:**

1️⃣ **Instant Lead Response** — Reply to inquiries in seconds, not hours. Even at 2 AM.

2️⃣ **Smart Intake Sequencing** — Automatically qualify prospects before they ever speak to a human. One firm saved 15 hours per week with this alone.

3️⃣ **Consultation Automation** — Scheduling, prep questionnaires, and multi-touch reminders that reduce no-shows by 60%.

4️⃣ **Nurture Campaigns** — Most prospects aren't ready to hire immediately. Stay top-of-mind without being annoying.

5️⃣ **Post-Case Follow-Up** — Your best marketing happens AFTER the case closes. Happy former clients refer friends and leave reviews.

**The biggest mistake?** Over-automating the human touch.

Automation should handle logistics, not relationships. The initial consultation and sensitive case discussions always need a human. Use automation to *enable* more personal attention, not replace it.
