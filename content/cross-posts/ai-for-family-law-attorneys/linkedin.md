AI won't replace family law attorneys. But attorneys who use AI will replace those who don't.

I built TextEvidence.ai after watching divorce attorneys spend 15+ hours manually reviewing client communications. That's not billable work—that's data entry wearing a suit.

Here are 8 practical AI applications transforming family law practice:

**1. Communication Analysis** 🎯 Highest impact
The average contested divorce: 10,000+ text messages. AI analyzes them in minutes, extracting relevant topics, identifying patterns, flagging concerning content.

Real result: Texas attorney found 47 instances of parental alienation that manual review missed. Pattern became central to custody argument.
Time saved: 12 hours → 45 minutes

**2. Legal Research**
Find jurisdiction-specific case law, summarize opinions, track emerging trends.
Real result: California attorney found 12 recent appellate decisions opposing counsel missed, including a 2024 statutory interpretation shift.
Time saved: 8 hours → 90 minutes

**3. Document Drafting**
Generate first drafts from case facts, adapt templates for local rules.
Time saved: 3 hours → 45 minutes of editing

**4. Financial Disclosure Analysis**
Identify spending patterns inconsistent with reported income, flag unexplained transfers.
Real result: Discovered recurring cash withdrawals indicating unreported cash business.
Time saved: 6 hours → 30 minutes

**5. Custody Timeline Construction**
Extract dates from communications, build chronological narratives judges love.
Time saved: 4 hours → 20 minutes

**6. Discovery Organization**
Categorize document dumps, create searchable indices.
Time saved: 10 hours → 1 hour

**7. Client Intake Screening**
Flag complexity indicators, identify conflicts early.

**8. Settlement Analysis**
Analyze similar cases for settlement ranges, evaluate offer strength.
Real result: Case settled 30% above initial offer after AI showed it was below median.

**⚠️ Common mistakes to avoid:**
❌ Expecting AI to replace attorney judgment
❌ Uploading client data to consumer AI tools (confidentiality violations!)
❌ Blind trust in AI output (hallucinations happen—verify everything)
❌ Using wrong tools for legal work
❌ Underestimating the learning curve

Start with communication analysis. Build competence. Expand from there.
